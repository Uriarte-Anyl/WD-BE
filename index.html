Perspective.

Anyl (Fibs) Imagines/ Designs/ and Creates. For him, a good design is not overwhelming and should be viewed effortlessly. Designing with intention is his objective in designs. He aims to craft user interactions through simple elements, shaping the style he aspires to follow

Approach.

Using the knowledge he got from searching and from his program Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, He experiments on designs and interactions. Minimal and subtle interactions is what he prefers to use on his designs. But he plans to upgrade the learning curve in his designs.

Self-evalutation.

There’s always more to learn when it comes to design. He knows his skills still need improvement, and with more practice, he hopes to create designs that truly connect with people. He also wants to get better at interaction design to bring out the full mood and meaning behind his work.

Inspirations.

Heavily inspired by Swiss design, he was fascinated by its clean and balanced approach. The simplicity of this style creates a sense of order, allowing viewers to engage effortlessly. He hopes to apply these principles in his future work by focusing on clarity and purposeful design.
